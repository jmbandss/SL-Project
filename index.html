Introduction

The Toy poodle is a dog breed that originates within North America and Germany in the 1900's. They are elegant-looking tiny dogs with a slender muzzle and long neck. Profuse and curly coat, oftenly styled by the owner of the dog. Toy Poodles have curly or wavy coats that can be soft or wiry, and come in many colors, including white, black, brown, red, cream, apricot, grey, and silver. They all grow to a specific size which is no more than 10 inches.
